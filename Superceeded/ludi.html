Ludi

I was born in 1992 close to Paris, but grew up in Auvergne (center of France). I started riding around 5 years old and then without the little back wheels at 6 y.o. I have a long passion for music and especially the saxophone and jazz, but my studies will then take most of my time.

Riding my bike at 7.

In Paris and in Nantes, she was not riding much, but during her academic travels (Ireland, Brazil and Chile), she discovered again the bike when commuting between Uni and home. When she met Alejandro in Nantes, the bike became much more important! She will then only use the bike to go from one place to another, or almost! Despite two bike accidents on the road, she dreams like a big bike trip and it will be in 2020! She is now a Dr, did a PhD in Bioengineering in Australia where she did a lot of bike promenades!
