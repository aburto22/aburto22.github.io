Welcome to our Les Petits Velos blog!

Hope you'll enjoy the blog and get some inspiration to start your own journey. We are a young vegetarian couple willing to enjoy our planet as much as possible but limiting our impact on it! We are a franco-chilean couple and we love our home countries. However, we wish to discover others before they change. Earth is so beautiful, every corner is a piece of art. We would like to discover and share what we see with you by words and photos on the blog and also with the people we will meet on our way.

Bon voyage!

Alejandro and Ludivine
