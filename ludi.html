Ludi

Riding my bike as a 7 year-old!

I was born in 1992 close to Paris but grew up in Auvergne (center of France). I started riding around 5 years old and then without the little back wheels at 6 y.o. I have a long passion for music and especially the saxophone and jazz, but my studies will then take most of my time.

In Paris and in Nantes, I was not riding much, but during my academic travels (Ireland, Brazil and Chile), I discovered again the bike when commuting between Uni and home. When I met Alejandro in Nantes, the bike became much more important! I will then only use the bike to go from one place to another, or almost! Despite two bike accidents on the road, I dream like a big bike trip! I am now a Dr, did a PhD in Bioengineering in Australia where I did a lot of bike promenades!
